About us

WHY HI-TECH?

Our staff is fully trained by British Pest Control Association (BPCA) and can be relied upon to deal with any pest control situation. Our technicians have up-to-date knowledge of techniques available for use in treating your home and business and will create customized treatment and maintenance programs for each customer. They carry out an obligation free Survey, a free Risk Assessment (COSHH) to identify existing and potential pest threats to your health and safety and so carry out the Integrated Pest Management programme for the welfare of your business and to save money loses caused by pests. Our staff is committed to provide professional, safe, friendly and efficient service to our highly valuable clients.

Free Survey

Our highly trained surveyor carry out a thorough obligation free survey of your premises to evaluate the type of pest, level of infestation and to find out the cause of the problem. The Surveyor will also provide you the advice and recommendations relating to hygiene, proofing and organising your stuff. This information is crucial to design the most effective treatment plan in line with the Integrated Pest Management (IPM) approach which suits your need as well as is cost effective.

Reporting

A comprehensive Report Folder will be left at each site which will comprises of a Report, a Risk Assessment (COSHH) and Recommendations sections. A treatment report sheet for every visit is left in this folder detailing all work carried out, observations made, pesticides used and recommendations made for client. In addition, a Checklist for the area covered in pest control contract and a list of Pesticides used are also provided in the Folder along with their active ingredients, concentration, formulation and their registration number. A Site Plan showing all toxic and non-toxic bait points and the insect monitoring traps can also be provided if required. This is all to keep you informed of our activities at your premises as well as to comply with the current Health and Safety Legislation. This Report folder is very important and should be kept safe to show due diligence to Environmental Health Officer (EHO). We are committed to maintain a safe and pest free environment to highest possible standards.
